Networking

Moving to another country is an exciting, difficult and complex experience. In addition to the main issues, such as adapting to a new culture, learning a new language, understanding the way of life, there are several factors that can help or hinder the absorption process.

One of the most important points is to build your new network. In our country, we often do not realize the importance of personal and professional contacts, as we have been building throughout our lives in different environments and most of us know who to turn to. As an immigrant your contact list is almost empty.

To build your network, you will need to actively meet new people.
